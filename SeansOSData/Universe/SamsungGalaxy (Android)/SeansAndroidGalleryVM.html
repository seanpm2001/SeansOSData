Context

During day 1 of this device, I decided to install every app that has ever been downloaded across the Android devices I have used, except for a few, which refused to install. This led to a nightmarish amount of apps (over 1300) and has put a heavy burden on me with archival work, as I have to document all this, and preserve the device data. The installation took about 5 hours.

The app data takes up about 106 gigabytes of space. This method also allowed me to play games that no longer are offered on the Google Play store, which includes but is not limited to: All the past Angry Birds games, SMALL STREET, Tiny Death Star.
